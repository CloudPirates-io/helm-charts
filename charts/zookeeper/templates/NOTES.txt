ZooKeeper has been deployed!

**Connect to your ZooKeeper cluster:**

  kubectl run zk-client --rm --tty -i --restart='Never' \
    --image bitnami/zookeeper:3.9.2-debian-11-r0 -- bash

  # Inside the pod:
  zkCli.sh -server {{ include "zookeeper.fullname" . }}:{{ .Values.service.port }}

**To upgrade this release:**

  helm upgrade <RELEASE_NAME> ./charts/zookeeper

**To uninstall/delete the release:**

  helm delete <RELEASE_NAME>
